# Atherosoft Homepage Concept

This document outlines the design and content strategy for the Atherosoft homepage, based on the company's identity as a cutting-edge medical AI startup.

## 1. Brand Identity & Design Language

**Core Identity**: "Precision Cardiology meets Advanced AI"
**Keywords**: Medical, Intelligent, Accurate, Dark-mode, Futuristic but Trustworthy.

### Visual Style
*   **Theme**: **Deep Dark Theme** (as requested).
    *   Backgrounds: Rich blacks (`#050505`) and deep grays (`#121212`) rather than flat black.
    *   Glassmorphism: Subtle translucent cards for content to add depth.
*   **Colors**:
    *   **Primary**: **Athero Purple** (Derived from `ci_generator` variable `purple`) - representing AI/Intelligence.
        *   Proposed hex: `#7c3aed` (Violet) or `#a855f7` (Purple).
    *   **Secondary**: **Life Green** (Derived from `ci_generator` variable `green`) - representing Biology/Healing.
        *   Proposed hex: `#10b981` (Emerald) or `#00ff9d` (Neon Green for accents).
    *   **Base Text**: White / Off-white for high readability on dark backgrounds.
*   **Typography**:
    *   Headings: Modern Sans-serif (e.g., 'Outfit' or 'Inter') - Clean, tech-focused.
    *   Body: Highly legible Sans-serif (e.g., 'Inter' or 'Roboto') - optimizing for medical data readability.

## 2. Content Strategy & Structure

### Section 1: Hero (First Impression)
*   **Headline**: "Unveiling the Invisible in Cardiovascular Health" or "Next-Gen IVUS Analysis with OMNIVUS"
*   **Sub-headline**: "Powered by AI, Validated by Cardiologists. Atherosoft revolutionizes vascular imaging analysis."
*   **Visual**: A stunning, abstract 3D visualization of a blood vessel or an stylized IVUS pullback, glowing against the dark background.
*   **CTA**: [Learn More] [Technology]

### Section 2: Product - OMNIVUS
*   **Focus**: The core software.
*   **Features**:
    *   Automated IVUS Image Analysis.
    *   Key Vessel Indicators Extraction (Lumen area, Plaque burden, etc.).
    *   Instant processing speed.
*   **Visual**: Mockup of the software interface showing analysis in action. High contrast, dark-mode UI.

### Section 3: Technology & Validation
*   **Focus**: Credibility.
*   **Content**:
    *   "Backed by Research": Mention "Numerous Papers & Patents".
    *   "Proprietary Algorithms": Highlight the AI engine.
*   **Proof**: Logos of journals or stylized icons representing patents.

### Section 4: About Us (The Team)
*   **Narrative**: The perfect synergy of Medicine and Engineering.
*   **Key Profiles**:
    *   **CEO**: Cardiologist background (Clinical needs expert).
    *   **CTO**: AI Scientist background (Deep Learning expert).
*   **Context**: Seed funded in Korea, growing globally.

*   **Links**: Contact, Careers, Privacy Policy.

## 3. Implementation Details
*   **Stack**: HTML5, Vanilla CSS (for custom dark theme control), Vanilla JS.
*   **Responsiveness**: Mobile-optimized (important for quick demos).
*   **Animations**:
