Our People

Bridging Medicine & AI

Founded by cardiologists and AI scientists, Atherosoft is dedicated to revolutionizing cardiovascular interventions through advanced imaging and precision analytics.

CEO

Soo-Jin Kang

Soo-Jin Kang is a Professor of Cardiology at Asan Medical Center, Seoul, with a Ph.D. in Medicine from Hanyang University and Ulsan University. She specializes in Coronary Imaging & Hemodynamics, and Echocardiography. Previously, she served as a Post-doctoral Research Fellow at Columbia University Medical Center, NY, and as an Invited Faculty at CRF IVUS Core Lab, NY. She is the Lead/Corresponding author of 60+ SCI papers in cardiovascular imaging and corresponding author of 9 SCI papers in cardiovascular AI. She holds 6 Korean and 4 Overseas patents.

CTO

Hyung Joo Cho

Hyung Joo Cho is a co-founder of Atherosoft. He holds a Ph.D. in Medical AI Engineering from Seoul National University. He is a former Researcher at LG Electronics, DeepBio, and Tomocube. He developed core models for cardiovascular imaging analysis (4 papers as lead author). He is the lead author of 9 papers and co-author of 30+ papers, including Nature Cell Biology and NeurIPS. He holds 10+ Korean and 3 Overseas patents. He is an expert in AI quality assessment, including Robustness and Uncertainty quantification, with extensive experience in deploying ML software across automotive, medical, and manufacturing sectors.

AI Lead

Sangwook Kim

Sangwook Kim holds a Bachelor's in Biomedical Engineering from Korea University and is a Ph.D. Candidate in Medical AI at University of Toronto. He is a former Researcher at Peter Munk Cardiac Centre, University Health Network, Canada, and currently works as an AI Researcher at Seoul National University Hospital and Severance Radiology. He is the lead author of 7 papers and 20+ conference papers, including MICCAI and Radiology AI. He specializes in cardiovascular multi-modal data analysis algorithms.

Dev Lead

Jihyeong Yoo

Jihyeong Yoo holds a Bachelor's in Computer Science from Yonsei University and a Master's in Computer Science from HKUST. He is a former Researcher at Tomocube and former AI Team Lead at Genesis Lab, where he led the acquisition of national AI reliability certification. He is the author of 7 papers, including ICML, EuroIntervention, and JACC intervention. He is experienced in numerous AI software development projects.
